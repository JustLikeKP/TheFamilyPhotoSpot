Recipe 1: Grandma's Apple Pie

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 large apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Pie crust (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the apples, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and lemon juice.
  3. Place the bottom crust in a pie pan and fill with the apple mixture.
  4. Dot the apples with butter and cover with the top crust.
  5. Cut slits in the top crust for ventilation and bake for 50-60 minutes, until golden brown.
  6. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
