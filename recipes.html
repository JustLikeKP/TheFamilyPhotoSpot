Nana's Famous Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter and sugars until smooth.
